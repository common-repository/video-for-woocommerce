Add a youtube video to your WooCommerce products.

== Description ==

Enhance your WooCommerce product pages by **embedding YouTube videos easily** and effectively. With WooCommerce Video, boost customer engagement by providing dynamic visual content.

You can choose between :

* First, second and last position of the video thumbnail
* Different video thumbnails

== Installation ==

1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
2. Set up the plugin into "Settings" > "Woocommerce Video"
3. Add the Youtube URL video into your WooCommerce product

== Usage ==

After installation, a new field will appear in the product data panel of each WooCommerce product. Here, you can:

* Enter YouTube URL: Simply paste the YouTube link to the video you want to display.
* Adjust Display Options: Choose where the video will appear on the product page.
